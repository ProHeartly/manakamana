From our humble beginnings to our current standing, we have remained dedicated to producing high-quality leaf plates that honor our heritage while serving the modern market. Every Duna and Tapari carries the spirit of Nepal's forests and the hands of our skilled craftspeople.

How It's Made

From Forest to Your Table

A simple, honest process — deeply rooted in tradition, 100% chemical-free, and crafted with pure dedication.

01

Leaf Collection

Fresh Sal leaves carefully collected from sustainable forest sources in Nepal's Terai region.

02

Cleaning & Drying

Leaves are washed with clean water and sun-dried to the right moisture level for pressing.

03

Pressing & Shaping

Leaves are layered and pressed using traditional molds to form the Tapari or Duna shape.

04

Quality Check

Every batch is inspected for shape, strength, and cleanliness before being packed.

05

Packed & Delivered

Orders are packed in bundles and dispatched to customers across Nepal and beyond.
